Additional pointers
Use the whiteboard judiciously. Start at the top left and work your way down, then right. Write as legibly as possible. Clearly separate pseudo-code from real code. Use generally-accepted coding conventions (in Java, for instance, method names have mixed case letters, beginning with a lower case letter and starting each subsequent word with an upper case letter). Make sure your parentheses and brackets are balanced, and you‚Äôre not missing a sea of semicolons in your functions.
